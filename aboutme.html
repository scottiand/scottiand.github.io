About Me

Hello! My name is Scotti Anderson. I was born and raised in the Pacific Northwest, eventually going to school in the heart of the Willamette Valley at Willamette University. There I found my passion for programming and recieved my Bachelor's in Computer Science. During my education, I had the privilage to work on a wide variety of projects, including several web applications and one cross-platform app, in addition to growing my skills as an artist and a human being.

I am self practiced in pixel art, a digital art form based on drawing images at the pixel level. The style was born from old video games, where art assets had to folow limitations in file size and color count. Now, pixel art is not done out of necessity, but for its unique style and retro feel.

Another important aspect of my life is game design. I am interested in creating board games, card games, and other tabletop gaming experiences. As a programmer, I am particularly interested in how technology can be integrated with tabletop experiences to to create new gameplay possibilities.

I am currently exploring oppurtunities in the Salem, Portland, or Eugene area to start my career as a software engineer.
